Funeral Benefits

The Funeral Plan provides for a cash payment of a death claim of a Principal Member, his/her Spouse, children, Extended Family Members and Wider Children, where applicable.

Funeral Package

The funeral package consists of the following benefits
  1. Single Benefit
  2. Family Benefit
  3. Optional Extended Family Benefit
  4. Senior Member Benefit
  5. Additional Optional Benefit, as Specified

Insured Persons Definitions

Principal Member: any Individual, who is a member of Pro Finance Plan, from Age 18 years, who is allowed to participate in the Policy, in terms of the eligibility conditions as stated in the Policy Document. The Maximum entry age for a Principal Member is 94 Years. A Principal Member must Live in South Africa. Spouse: A person married to the Principal Member by Law or tribal custom or under the tenents of any Asian religion, which shall include a Common law Spouse of the Principal Member.

Spouse

A person married to the principal Member by law or tribal custom or under the tenets of any Aspian religion, which shall include common law Spouseof the principal Member.The maximum entry age for a spouse is 65 years.only a maximum of two spouses may be covered per policy.

Common Law Spouse

A person who is deemed by Pro Finance,at its sole discreation,to be a spouse,considering the circumstances of each case,and shall include,where applicable,costomary marriages,or a relationship between two people of the same gender, or relationship between two people who have lived together for at least six consecutive months prior to the date of death of the spouse.

Child

An unmarried child of the principal Member,younger than age 21 years,including a stepchild, posthumus child, an illegitimate child,a legally adopted child or stillborn child(must be stillborn from the 26th week of pregnancy).only 2 stillbirth claims will be accepted per family durung the term of the policy. cover of children is extended to under age 26 years if the child is a full-time student at a recognised educational institution.this does not include part-time and correspondence students.children who a mentally disabled or totally and permanently disabled before at age of 21 years,who are unable to care for themselves,are covered until cover for the principal Member ends.Details of any children of a common law spouse,illegitimate chid and stepchild must be supplied to Pro Finance at the time that the principal Member joins the scheme,or within 1 month of the child becoming eligible for cover.failing this,Pro Finance will require satisfactory proof to support any claim.

Optional Extended Family

Family members,who are dependent on the principal member for financial assistance in the event of their death,may be covered.these include parents,parents in-law,uncles,aunts brothers,sisters,nephews,nieces,grandparents and children(who are age 22 years and older)of the pricipal Member.

Please Note

no maximum age fro extended family policy applications up to extended family may be covered. only in case of marriage may extended famly(parent in-law) be added,provided the maximum number of extended family members has not been exceeded(approval to be granted by Pro Finance before premiums are amended). extended family members are not nominated more tha once by the same principal Member and the maximum benefit limit if R60 000is not exceeded for such extended family members across aa Pro Finance plans.

Dependants

Means a spouse,children,extended family members and wider child,where applicable
